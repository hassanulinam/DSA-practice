# DSA Practice Plan - Week 9-10

## Interview Preparation

### Week 9 Goals:
1. Simulate real interview conditions
2. Practice whiteboard coding
3. Review all problems solved
4. Begin behavioral preparation

### Week 10 Goals:
1. Complete mock interviews
2. Final review of all topics
3. Prepare for system design questions
4. Final practice and confidence building

### Interview Preparation Activities:

#### 1. Mock Interviews (Week 9-10)
- Schedule 2-3 mock interviews per week
- Use platforms like Pramp, Interviewing.io, or LeetCode mock interviews
- Practice explaining your thought process
- Time yourself strictly (45-60 minutes per problem)
- Get feedback on approach and communication

#### 2. Whiteboard Practice (Week 9)
- Practice solving problems without IDE
- Explain your approach clearly
- Write clean, readable code
- Discuss time and space complexity
- Handle edge cases

#### 3. Problem Review (Week 9-10)
- Review all problems solved in previous weeks
- Create summary notes for each topic
- Identify weak areas and revisit them
- Document different approaches to same problems
- Create flashcards for important concepts

#### 4. Behavioral Preparation (Week 10)
- Prepare answers to common behavioral questions
- Practice the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Review your resume and projects
- Prepare questions to ask the interviewer

### Final Review Topics:

#### 1. Core Concepts Review
- Time and space complexity analysis
- Data structures (arrays, strings, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, graphs)
- Algorithm techniques (two pointers, sliding window, recursion, backtracking, dynamic programming, BFS/DFS)
- Common patterns and problem-solving approaches

#### 2. Key Problem Types
- Array/string manipulation
- Tree and graph traversal
- Sorting and searching algorithms
- Dynamic programming problems
- Bit manipulation problems
- Mathematical problems

#### 3. System Design Concepts (for senior roles)
- Scalability principles
- Load balancing
- Caching strategies
- Database design
- API design

### Success Metrics:
1. Complete 10+ mock interviews
2. Solve 200+ problems across different categories
3. Achieve 80%+ accuracy on LeetCode problems
4. Be able to explain algorithms clearly
5. Understand and optimize solutions for time and space complexity
6. Feel confident in both technical and behavioral aspects

### Final Tips:
1. Practice daily, even if just 1-2 problems
2. Focus on understanding, not memorization
3. Explain your approach out loud
4. Time yourself during practice
5. Join DSA study groups for motivation and support
6. Don't neglect your resume and portfolio projects
7. Prepare questions to ask interviewers
8. Stay consistent and patient with the process
