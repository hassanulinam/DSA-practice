# DSA Study Schedule Template

## Weekly Structure

### Monday
- 2 Easy problems
- 1 Medium problem

### Tuesday
- 2 Easy problems
- 1 Medium problem

### Wednesday
- 2 Medium problems
- 1 Hard problem

### Thursday
- 2 Medium problems
- 1 Hard problem

### Friday
- 1 Hard problem
- Review of week's problems

### Saturday
- 1 Hard problem
- Review of week's problems

### Sunday
- Rest/Review

## Daily Problem Breakdown

### Easy Problems (30-45 minutes each)
- Focus on understanding the approach
- Practice writing clean code
- Discuss time and space complexity

### Medium Problems (45-60 minutes each)
- Apply multiple concepts
- Practice optimization techniques
- Discuss edge cases

### Hard Problems (60-90 minutes each)
- Deep dive into complex algorithms
- Practice breaking down problems
- Learn new techniques

## Progress Tracking

### Weekly Goals
1. Solve 10-15 problems per week
2. Complete 2-3 mock interviews per month
3. Review all solved problems
4. Document learning and insights

### Monthly Goals
1. Complete 4 weeks of practice
2. Solve 40-60 problems
3. Master 1-2 new concepts
4. Improve problem-solving speed

## Study Tips

1. **Consistency is Key**: Daily practice is more effective than sporadic long sessions
2. **Understand, Don't Memorize**: Focus on understanding the logic behind solutions
3. **Explain Out Loud**: Practice articulating your approach to problems
4. **Time Yourself**: Simulate real interview conditions
5. **Review Regularly**: Go back to problems you've solved to reinforce learning
6. **Join Communities**: Participate in DSA study groups for motivation and support
